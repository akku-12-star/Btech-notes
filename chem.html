Chemistry

Module-wise Syllabus Breakdown

Module 1

  • Atomic and molecular structure
  • Molecular orbitals of diatomic molecules
  • Crystal field theory – Crystal field splitting in octahedral and tetrahedral complexes
  • Band structure of solids and the role of doping on band structures

Module 2

  • Water Technology – Hardness of water and its estimation by EDTA method
  • Boiler feed water – boiler problems – priming and foaming, scale and sludge, caustic embrittlement
  • Water treatment – Internal treatment and external treatment
  • Desalination of brackish water – Reverse osmosis, electrodialysis

Module 3

  • Electrochemistry – Galvanic cells, Electrode potential, Electrochemical series
  • Nernst equation – Reference electrodes – SHE, Calomel and Glass electrode
  • Battery Technology – primary and secondary cells
  • Fuel cells – H2-O2 fuel cell, Li-ion cell
  • Corrosion – types, mechanisms and methods of prevention

Module 4

  • Spectroscopy and analytical techniques
  • Beer–Lambert’s law – UV–Visible spectroscopy and its applications
  • Infrared spectroscopy and its applications
  • Chromatography – principle and applications of paper chromatography and thin layer chromatography
