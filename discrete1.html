Discrete Mathematics

Module-wise Syllabus Breakdown

Module 1

  • Sets and subsets – operations on sets – set identities
  • Venn Diagrams – ordered pairs – Cartesian product
  • Relations – properties of relations
  • Representing relations – Closures of relations
  • Equivalence relations – Partial orderings

Module 2

  • Functions – one-to-one, onto and bijective functions
  • Inverse and composition of functions – recursive functions
  • Graphs of functions – floor and ceiling functions
  • Algebraic structures – semigroups, monoids and groups
  • Subgroups – cyclic groups – homomorphism of groups

Module 3

  • Propositions – logical operators – truth tables
  • Equivalence – implications – laws of logic
  • Rules of inference – quantifiers – nested quantifiers
  • Normal forms – proof techniques

Module 4

  • Introduction to Graph Theory – basic terminology
  • Simple graph, multigraph and pseudograph
  • Graph representations – degree – types of graphs
  • Subgraphs – complete, regular, bipartite graphs
  • Graph isomorphism – paths and connectivity
  • Euler and Hamiltonian paths and circuits
